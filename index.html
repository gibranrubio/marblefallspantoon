About

Built For Easy Lake Days

Marble Falls Pontoon Rentals is a simple, friendly pontoon rental service for anyone who wants a relaxed day on Lake Marble Falls without the hassle of owning a boat.

Bring your group, your cooler, your sunscreen, and your playlist. We’ll help get you set up for a clean, comfortable lake day.

The Lake

Lake Marble Falls

Lake Marble Falls is one of the best places in the Hill Country for an easy pontoon day. Cruise, swim, relax, and enjoy the water with your favorite people.

Great For

Family days, birthday weekends, bachelor and bachelorette groups, sunset cruises, and slow afternoons on the water.

Bring

Towels, sunscreen, snacks, drinks, sunglasses, and a valid ID for the renter.
