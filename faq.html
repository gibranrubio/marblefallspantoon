FAQ

Q: Where are rentals located?
A: Rentals are for Marble Falls and Lake Marble Falls. Exact launch and meet-up details can be confirmed when booking.

Q: How long is a rental?
A: A 3-hour minimum is recommended for most pontoon rentals. Longer blocks are available for half-day or full-day outings.

Q: What is required to make a reservation?
A: The reserving person should have a valid ID and credit card. A deposit may be required to lock in the reservation.

Q: Is a captain available?
A: Yes, a captain can be available upon request depending on date, time, and availability.

Q: What should we bring?
A: Towels, sunscreen, drinks, snacks, and anything you need for a comfortable lake day. Life jackets and basic safety gear should be available on the boat.

Q: What is the cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations and weather reschedules can be handled case-by-case. The goal is to keep the booking simple and fair.

Q: What if unfavorable weather is predicted?
A: Forecasted rain does not always mean the rental has to cancel. If weather creates unsafe lake conditions at the time of the outing, rescheduling can be discussed.
